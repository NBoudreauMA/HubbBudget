🏛 Town Administrator’s Message

Town of Hubbardston Fiscal Year 2026 Budget Executive Summary

Introduction: The Fiscal Year 2026 (FY26) budget for the Town of Hubbardston reflects our continued commitment to responsible financial stewardship while investing in essential services that support our residents. This budget balances fiscal responsibility with the need to maintain and enhance core services, improve municipal operations, and ensure the town’s long-term sustainability. With rising costs and evolving community needs, this year’s budget is designed to align with strategic goals, emphasizing efficiency, transparency, and prudent planning.

Revenue Overview

The FY26 budget projects total revenue of $11,699,616, representing a 5.71% increase from FY25. The revenue sources include:

  • Tax Levy: $9,276,260, accounting for the largest share of revenue, inclusive of Proposition 2.5 adjustments and new growth.
  • State Aid: $730,906, reflecting modest increases in unrestricted government aid and local reimbursement programs.
  • Local Receipts: $1,619,685, generated through excise taxes, permit fees, and other municipal revenues.
  • Other Sources: Free Cash allocations and stabilization transfers strategically used to support capital needs and operational costs.

Expenditure Summary

The proposed FY26 budget totals $11,848,376, a 6.29% increase from FY25. Key allocations include:

  • General Government: $728,886 - Investments in IT modernization, administrative support, and digital engagement.
  • Public Safety: $1,581,842 - Fire and Police investments, body cameras, and regional partnerships.
  • Public Works: $920,184 - Infrastructure repairs, road maintenance, and DPW investments.
  • Education: $7,334,563 - School assessments, funding advocacy, and stability planning.
  • Human Services & Culture: $121,239 - Senior services, library programs, and recreation funding.
  • Debt Service & Liabilities: $1,151,810 - Managing existing debt and planning for future liabilities.

Capital Investments & Free Cash Utilization

A total of $525,000 in Free Cash allocations is proposed to support:

  • 🚧 Capital Improvement Projects: $275,000 - Focused on facility repairs and infrastructure.
  • 💰 General Stabilization Fund: $10,000 - Enhancing financial reserves.
  • 🔧 Operating Budget Support: $130,000 - Addressing rising fixed costs.
  • 💻 Technology Enhancements: Investments in automation and Microsoft 365 integration.

Key Challenges & Considerations

  • Rising Costs: Inflation and contractual wage adjustments.
  • Financial Sustainability: Strategic revenue growth and grant opportunities.
  • Education Funding: Continued advocacy for fair and sustainable school funding.
  • Transition to 48 Gardner Road: Optimizing the new Town Hall space.

Conclusion

The FY26 budget is a forward-thinking plan that ensures Hubbardston remains financially stable while addressing key operational needs. Despite fiscal challenges, the town's **proactive planning, strategic investments, and commitment to transparency** will continue to provide high-quality services to residents.
